Hieee! I'm Sreekar. After years of just mixing instant coffee and calling it a day, I finally grabbed an Aeropress... and whoosh, down the rabbit hole I went!

Now, I'm experimenting with grind sizes, tweaking water temperatures, chasing flavours, and making a million mistakes along the way.

This blog is where I spill it all—my coffee logs (how each cup turned out) and my coffee notes (all the nerdy coffee stuff I learn).

You'll also find stories of my café visits, favorite coffee books, and plenty more coffee ramblings.

I'm no coffee expert, just someone who's super curious and excited to figure things out, one cup at a time.

So grab your favorite mug and join me, let’s go exploring together on this caffeinated adventure! ☕️
